# Privacy Policy for BELVRR

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

BELVRR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Birthdate**: When you use BELVRR, you enter a birthdate to generate life statistics. In the current version, this information is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, such as which features are used most frequently.
- **Device Information**: Basic device information may be collected for app optimization purposes.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain our service
- Calculate and display your life statistics
- Improve and optimize our app
- Communicate with you about updates (if you opt-in)

## Data Storage and Security

### Current Version (V1)

- All calculations are performed locally on your device
- No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers
- Your birthdate is not saved between sessions unless you choose to save it locally

### Future Versions

When we introduce account features:

- Data will be stored securely using Firebase services
- You will have the ability to delete your account and all associated data
- We will update this policy accordingly

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase** (Google): For hosting and future authentication
- **Apple Services**: For App Store distribution and Sign in with Apple
- **Google Services**: For Sign in with Google (future feature)

## Children's Privacy

BELVRR is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of communications
- Export your data (future feature)

## Data Retention

- Local data: Retained until you clear app data or uninstall
- Server data (future): Retained until account deletion

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

- Email: privacy@belvrr.com
- Website: https://belvrr.com

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights regarding their personal information. Please contact us for more information.

## International Users

By using BELVRR, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States.
